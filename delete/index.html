Delete Your Account

Last updated: June 22, 2026

This page explains how to delete your SnugTales account and the data associated with it. SnugTales is operated by The Lifetime Value Co.

Delete your account from the app

  1. Open the SnugTales app and sign in.
  2. Go to the Family tab (Settings).
  3. Scroll to Account and tap Delete my account.
  4. Confirm. Your account and all associated data are permanently deleted.

Prefer to email us?

You can also request deletion by emailing support@snugtalesapp.com from the address associated with your account. We will delete your account and data promptly.

What gets deleted

When you delete your account, we permanently remove:

  • Your account and email address
  • Your children's profiles (names, ages, interests, notes)
  • Every story you've created, along with saved audio
  • Your story-generation history and any referral records
  • Stories you shared with others, and stories shared with you

What may be retained

We do not retain your personal content after deletion. Limited transaction records required for legal, tax, or accounting purposes may be retained by our payment processors (Apple or Google) as required by law. Account data is deleted immediately upon request; any residual backups are purged within 30 days.
