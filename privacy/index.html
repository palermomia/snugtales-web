Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 12, 2026

SnugTales ("we," "our," or "us") operates the SnugTales mobile application. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

Information We Collect

Account information: When you create an account, we collect your email address and a password (stored securely and never in plain text).

Family profile: To personalize stories, you may provide your children's first names, ages, and story preferences (themes, characters, moral lessons). This information is provided voluntarily by you, the parent or guardian.

Usage data: We collect basic logs of app activity (e.g. stories generated, features used) to improve the service.

How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your account
  • To generate personalized bedtime stories for your children
  • To remember your preferences and story history
  • To communicate service updates or account information

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for advertising or marketing purposes.

Children's Privacy

SnugTales is designed for use by parents and guardians on behalf of their children. We do not knowingly collect personal information directly from children under 13. All accounts are created and managed by adults. Children's names and ages entered into the app are used solely to personalize stories and are not shared with third parties.

Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely with industry-standard encryption in transit (HTTPS) and at rest. We retain your data for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion at any time.

Your Rights

You may at any time:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your account and all associated data

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@snugtalesapp.com.

Third-Party Services

To generate your stories, story prompts and the child profile details you provide may be sent to third-party AI providers. They use this information solely to generate the requested story — not to train their models or for any other purpose. We do not share your information with any other third parties for advertising or marketing.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We'll notify you of significant changes via email or in-app notice.
