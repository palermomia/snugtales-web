Features

Stories as unique
as your children

🌙

Personalised every night

Each story is freshly generated for your kids — using their names, ages, and what happened in their day. No two nights are the same.

✨

Choose your adventure

Pick from adventure, fantasy, mystery, science, or cozy stories. Short, medium, or long — whatever fits bedtime tonight.

💛

Sneak in today

Tell us what happened today — a trip to the park, a funny moment — and watch it magically appear in tonight's story.

📚

Story library

Every story is saved to your library so you can re-read favourites. Kids always have a bedtime story, even offline.

🌟

A little lesson, never preachy

Optionally weave in a moral — kindness, courage, honesty — and the story will live it out naturally, never lecture it.

👨‍👩‍👧‍👦

Built for families

Add multiple kids with different ages and preferences. Stories are always age-appropriate and perfectly calibrated.

How it works

Bedtime in three taps

1

Set up your family

Add your kids' names and ages once. SnugTales remembers everything so every night starts instantly.

2

Customise tonight

Choose who's listening, pick a story type, add what happened today. Takes about 30 seconds.

3

Read together

A brand-new story appears in moments. Read it aloud, snuggle up, and drift off into the adventure.
