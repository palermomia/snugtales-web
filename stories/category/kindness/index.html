Frequently asked questions

How do bedtime stories teach kindness?

Stories let children see kindness in action and feel its rewards through a character they care about — a gentler, stickier lesson than simply being told to be kind.

Do these stories come with discussion questions?

Yes. Every story ends with a simple moral and a few parent discussion questions you can use to talk about it together at bedtime.

What ages are moral bedtime stories for?

They suit roughly ages 3–7. Each story lists its age range so you can choose what fits your child.
