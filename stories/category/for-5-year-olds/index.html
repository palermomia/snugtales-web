Frequently asked questions

What do 5 year olds like in a bedtime story?

A real little plot: a problem, a brave or kind choice, and a satisfying ending. They also love spotting the moral — each story here ends with one to talk about.

How long should a bedtime story be for a 5 year old?

Around 5–8 minutes works well. Every story lists its reading time, and this page only shows stories suited to age 5.

Are these stories free?

Yes — free to read online. In the free SnugTales app, any story can star your 5 year old by name.
