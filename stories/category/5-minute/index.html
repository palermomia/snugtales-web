Frequently asked questions

Are 5-minute stories long enough for bedtime?

Yes — a complete 5-minute story is often the sweet spot: long enough to wind down, short enough to keep on a late or busy night without a battle.

Are these full stories or just short excerpts?

Every one is a complete tale with a beginning, middle, and calm ending — just written to read comfortably in about five minutes.

Do they work for tired parents too?

That’s the idea. Short, self-contained, and soothing — and in the app you can have any of them narrated so you can rest your voice.
