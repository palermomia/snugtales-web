Frequently asked questions

What makes a good bedtime story for a 3 year old?

Simple sentences, a friendly main character, a little repetition, and a calm ending. Around 4–6 minutes is the sweet spot at this age.

How long should bedtime stories be for a 3 year old?

About 4–6 minutes. Every story here lists its reading time, and this page only shows stories suited to age 3.

Are these stories really free?

Yes — free to read online. The free SnugTales app can also personalize any story so your 3 year old is the hero.
