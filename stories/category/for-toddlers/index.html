Frequently asked questions

What makes a good bedtime story for a toddler?

Keep it short (3–5 minutes), use simple repetitive language, and end calmly. Toddlers settle best with gentle, predictable stories that wind down rather than excite.

How long should a toddler bedtime story be?

Around 3–5 minutes is ideal for ages 1–3. Every story in this collection lists its reading time so you can pick one that fits your night.

Are these stories free to read?

Yes — every story here is free to read online. In the SnugTales app you can also turn any story into a personalized tale starring your own child.
