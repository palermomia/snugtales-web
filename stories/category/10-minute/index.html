Frequently asked questions

When is a 10-minute story the right choice?

When bedtime isn’t rushed and your child wants a "real" story — a full adventure with room to breathe. The longer arc also gives more time to physically wind down.

What age suits 10-minute bedtime stories?

Usually ages 4–8, when attention spans enjoy a longer tale. Each story lists its age range.
