Frequently asked questions

Why do kids love animal bedtime stories?

Animal characters let children explore big feelings — bravery, kindness, sharing — from a safe, friendly distance, which makes the lessons easier to take in at bedtime.

What ages are these animal stories for?

Most suit ages 2–7. Each story is tagged with an age range and reading time so you can match it to your child.

Can I make my child the star of an animal story?

Yes — in the free SnugTales app, any story here can be personalized so your child becomes the hero alongside the animal characters.
