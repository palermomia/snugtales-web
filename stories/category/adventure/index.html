Frequently asked questions

Won’t an adventure story make my child too excited for bed?

Not these. Each adventure builds gently and always lands on a calm, sleepy ending — the excitement fades into coziness, so your child winds down instead of winding up.

What age are adventure bedtime stories best for?

They work well for ages 3–7, when imaginations are growing. Every story shows its age range and reading time.

Are the stories free?

Yes, all free to read online. The SnugTales app can also personalize any adventure so your child is the hero of the journey.
